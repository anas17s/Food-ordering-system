-ABOUT US-
Welcome to KCT Food Ordering Portal. This online platform is created in motive to save your wasteful time that you spend in KCT Canteens. You can able to order your food from this website and make your food be prepared. Once the food has been ordered from this website, you will be getting an order ID. The generated order ID should be typed in the Food Cart so that you can take the hardcopy of the ordered bill. This bill can be used to get your food inside the Canteen
